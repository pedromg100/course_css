Types of units

  1. Absolute units
    • Pixels (px): 10px text
    • Points (pt) - 1 pt = 1.33 px: 10pt text
    • Pica (pc) - 1 pc = 12 pts: 1pc text
    • Centimeters (cm): 0.5cm text
    • Milimeters (mm): 5mm text
  2. Relative units
    • Percentage (%) - relative to the parent value: 100% text
    • EM (em) - relative to the standard font size: 1em text
    • Volume width (vw) - 1 vw = 1% of volume width: 3vw text
    • Volume height (vh) - 1 vh = 1% of volume height: 3vh text
